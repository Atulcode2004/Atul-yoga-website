Weekly Yoga Class Schedule

Our free yoga course includes daily classes focused on different aspects of yoga, from strength-building to relaxation and mindfulness.

Monday: Beginner Yoga Flow

Start your week by learning the basics of yoga, focusing on fundamental postures and breathwork. This class is perfect for beginners looking to build a strong foundation.

  • 7:00 AM - 60 mins
  • Gentle stretches, basic asanas, and breath awareness
  • Focus on alignment and mindfulness

Tuesday: Breathwork & Meditation

Enhance your mental clarity and calm your mind with guided breathwork and meditation. This class is designed to reduce stress and boost mental focus.

  • 6:30 PM - 45 mins
  • Breathing techniques like Nadi Shodhana
  • Guided meditation for relaxation

Wednesday: Vinyasa Flow

Flow through dynamic movements in this energizing class, building strength and flexibility while syncing breath with motion.

  • 7:00 AM - 60 mins
  • Sun Salutations and standing poses
  • Increase flexibility, build endurance

Thursday: Strength & Flexibility

This class focuses on building both strength and flexibility, using a mix of yoga poses to challenge your muscles while increasing your range of motion.

  • 6:30 PM - 60 mins
  • Strength-building poses like Plank, Warrior
  • Deep flexibility stretches

Friday: Restorative Yoga

End your week with deep relaxation, holding passive poses for longer periods. This restorative class promotes healing and rejuvenation.

  • 7:00 AM - 60 mins
  • Restorative poses using props for support
  • Deep breathing to activate the parasympathetic nervous system

Saturday: Power Yoga

Build strength, flexibility, and endurance with this high-energy, fast-paced yoga class designed to challenge your physical limits.

  • 8:00 AM - 60 mins
  • Intense poses to build strength and stamina
  • Cardio-focused movements

Sunday: Yoga for Stress Relief

End your week with a calming and restorative class designed to release stress and tension. Perfect for preparing for the week ahead.

  • 6:00 PM - 45 mins
  • Gentle stretches, relaxation poses
  • Guided meditation to reduce stress
