What is Hatha Yoga?

Hatha Yoga is a traditional form of yoga that focuses on physical postures (asanas), breathing techniques (pranayama), and meditation. It aims to bring balance to the body and mind, and is a foundation for many modern yoga practices.

Benefits of Hatha Yoga

  • Improves posture and body alignment
  • Enhances flexibility and strength
  • Supports stress management and relaxation
  • Stimulates mental clarity and focus
  • Promotes energy balance and vitality

Common Hatha Yoga Poses

Hatha Yoga includes a variety of gentle poses designed to increase awareness and prepare the body for meditation. Some commonly practiced poses are:

  • Mountain Pose (Tadasana)
  • Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)
  • Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)
  • Bridge Pose (Setu Bandhasana)
  • Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana)
