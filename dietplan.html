7-Day Yoga Diet Plan

Boost your wellness journey with this balanced 7-day vegetarian yoga diet. Each day is carefully crafted to support energy, digestion, and mindfulness. Simple, effective, and easy to follow!

Day 1 - Detox & Hydration
  • Morning: Warm lemon water, soaked almonds, light stretching
  • Breakfast: Oats with banana and flax seeds
  • Lunch: Steamed brown rice, dal, mixed salad
  • Evening Snack: Coconut water & nuts
  • Dinner: Clear vegetable soup with quinoa
Day 2 - Energy Boost
  • Morning: Herbal tea with tulsi & ginger
  • Breakfast: Moong dal chilla with mint chutney
  • Lunch: Chapati, sabzi, cucumber raita
  • Evening Snack: Fruit salad
  • Dinner: Khichdi with veggies and a spoon of ghee
Day 3 - Light & Clean
  • Morning: Cumin water, 5 soaked raisins
  • Breakfast: Smoothie (spinach, banana, almond milk)
  • Lunch: Millet roti, sabzi, carrot-beet salad
  • Evening Snack: Roasted chana
  • Dinner: Pumpkin soup with whole grain toast
Day 4 - Strength & Stamina
  • Morning: Green tea & 3 walnuts
  • Breakfast: Poha with veggies and peanuts
  • Lunch: Rajma chawal with curd
  • Evening Snack: Buttermilk
  • Dinner: Grilled tofu with saut√©ed greens
Day 5 - Balanced Body
  • Morning: Lemon water + chia seeds
  • Breakfast: Upma with veggies
  • Lunch: Chapati, tofu bhurji, salad
  • Evening Snack: Green smoothie
  • Dinner: Miso soup with brown rice
Day 6 - Cleanse & Glow
  • Morning: Detox water (cucumber + mint)
  • Breakfast: Fresh papaya + sunflower seeds
  • Lunch: Quinoa bowl with chickpeas & veggies
  • Evening Snack: Herbal tea with jaggery
  • Dinner: Zucchini stir-fry with lentils
Day 7 - Reflection & Rejuvenation
  • Morning: Warm turmeric water & meditation
  • Breakfast: Idli with coconut chutney
  • Lunch: Masoor dal, rice, spinach sabzi
  • Evening Snack: Handful of dates & herbal tea
  • Dinner: Broccoli soup with millet bread
